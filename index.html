Mapping the "Invisible Air"

In Japan, communication isn't just about what is said; it is about Kyoushi (空気を読む)—the art of "Reading the Air." Social harmony (Wa) is maintained through a delicate balance between Tatemae (the polite facade) and Honne (the true feeling).
